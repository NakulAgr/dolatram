How to Order

Steps to Place Your Order

  1. 1 Select the product quantity or make a list of products and send it to us via WhatsApp or Email.
  2. 2 We will send you the total price within a few minutes.
  3. 3 Confirm your order by replying with "Yes" or "No".
  4. 4 If "No", your request will be deleted. If "Yes", you need to make the payment and confirm the order.
  5. 5 After confirming with "Yes" and making the payment, send us the shipping address.
